# Phoenix Wholesale Offer Calculator

## Research Summary

### MAO Formula (Maximum Allowable Offer)
The standard wholesale formula:
**MAO = (ARV × 70%) - Repair Costs - Assignment Fee**

- **70% Rule**: Industry standard for wholesaling
- **ARV**: After Repair Value - what property sells for after renovation
- **Repair Costs**: Estimated rehab expenses
- **Assignment Fee**: Your wholesale profit ($5,000-$20,000 typical)

### Rehab Estimator (Per Square Foot)
Based on 2026 national data:
- **Light/Cosmetic**: $25-$40/sq ft (paint, flooring, fixtures)
- **Medium/Value-Add**: $50-$75/sq ft (kitchen, baths, systems)
- **Heavy/Full Gut**: $90-$135+/sq ft (down to studs, full MEP)

### Phoenix-Specific Comps Sources
1. **Redfin** - Free comps, sold data
2. **Zillow** - Zestimate + sold listings
3. **Maricopa County Assessor** - Public records
4. **PropStream** - Investor-grade (paid)
5. **BatchLeads** - Investor data (paid)

### Phoenix Market Context (2026)
- Median home price: ~$462K
- Market: Somewhat competitive
- Average days on market: ~62 days
- Phoenix investors often use 70-75% ARV depending on competition

## Calculator Features

### Input Section
1. Property Address
2. Square Footage
3. ARV (After Repair Value)
4. Rehab Level (Light/Medium/Heavy)
5. Assignment Fee Target
6. ARV Discount % (default 70%)

### Output Section
1. Estimated Repair Costs
2. Maximum Allowable Offer (MAO)
3. Suggested Offer Range (85-90% of MAO)
4. Total Profit Breakdown
5. Deal Grade (A/B/C/D)

### Phoenix-Specific Additions
1. Comps lookup links (Redfin/Zillow)
2. Maricopa County records link
3. Local contractor cost adjustment (+5% for Phoenix labor)
4. Wholesale buyer network contacts section
